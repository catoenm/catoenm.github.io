epsilon-harmony

An application developed to take the piano into the virtual world. Using the Leap Motion and the Oculus Rift, players are brought together in a virtual environment enabling them to play duets on the same piano from anywhere on the planet. This project placed second overall at Hack Western, a major league hacking event. More info can be found here

2Stack

An android game developed to put strategic thinking and reflexes to the test. 2Stack combines the intuitition of the popular game 2048 with the fast paced gameplay of Tetris.

2Stack is Currently on the Google Play store available for free. The game can be found and downloaded here
